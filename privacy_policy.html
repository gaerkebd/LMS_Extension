Privacy Policy

Canvas LMS Time Estimator — Chrome Extension

Effective date: May 6, 2026

Short version: Canvas LMS Time Estimator does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data. Everything stays on your device unless you explicitly configure a third-party service.

1. Overview

Canvas LMS Time Estimator ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension that helps students estimate the time required to complete Canvas LMS assignments. This policy explains what information the Extension accesses, how that information is stored, and when — if ever — it is shared with third parties.

2. What Data Is Accessed and Stored

The Extension accesses only the data that is necessary to provide its features:

  • Canvas LMS URL and API token — You provide your own Canvas instance URL and a personal API token. These are stored in chrome.storage.sync (synced across your Chrome profile) and are used solely to fetch your assignment data from Canvas on your behalf.
  • Assignment data — Assignment titles, descriptions, due dates, and point values retrieved from the Canvas REST API are cached in chrome.storage.local on your device to generate time estimates.
  • Time estimates and usage counters — Generated time estimates and internal usage counters (e.g., number of estimates run) are stored in chrome.storage.local on your device.
  • OpenAI API key (optional) — If you choose to enable AI-powered estimates, you may supply your own OpenAI API key. It is stored in chrome.storage.sync and is sent only to OpenAI's servers when you trigger an AI estimate. The developer never receives this key.
  • Google account (optional, premium) — If you enable Google Calendar integration, the Extension uses Chrome's built-in OAuth2 flow (chrome.identity) to request limited calendar access. No Google credentials are stored by the Extension itself.

3. How Data Is Stored

All data described above is stored exclusively using the Chrome browser's built-in storage APIs (chrome.storage.sync and chrome.storage.local). This means:

  • Data stays on your device (and within your Chrome profile if sync is enabled).
  • The developer has no server-side access to any of your stored data.
  • You can clear all stored data at any time by removing the Extension from Chrome, or by using Chrome's “Clear Extension Storage” option.

4. Third-Party Services

The Extension may interact with the following third-party services. All interactions are opt-in — none are active unless you explicitly configure them.

  • Canvas LMS (your institution) — The Extension calls your institution's Canvas REST API using the token you provide. Your institution's own privacy policy governs that data.
  • OpenAI (optional) — If you supply an OpenAI API key and request an AI-generated estimate, assignment text is sent to OpenAI for processing. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy .
  • Ollama (local) (optional) — If you configure a local Ollama model, all processing happens on your own machine at localhost. No data leaves your device via this path.
  • Stripe — Subscription status for premium features is validated through the Extension's backend API. Only an anonymous subscription identifier is exchanged; payment details are handled entirely by Stripe and never pass through the Extension. See Stripe's Privacy Policy .
  • Google Calendar (optional, premium) — If you enable calendar integration, the Extension requests permission to create calendar events. Google's Privacy Policy applies to that interaction.

5. What Is Not Collected

The Extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit any of the following.

  • No analytics or usage telemetry sent to the developer.
  • No browsing history or activity outside of Canvas LMS pages.
  • No personally identifiable information (name, email, student ID, etc.).
  • No advertising identifiers or tracking pixels.
  • No crash reports or diagnostics sent off-device.
  • No data sold or shared with advertisers or data brokers, ever.

6. Browser Notifications

The Extension may display browser notifications to alert you about upcoming or urgent assignments. These notifications are generated entirely on your device from locally cached assignment data. No data is transmitted to produce them.

7. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the Extension, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, the effective date above will be updated and the new version will be published at the same URL. Continued use of the Extension after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
